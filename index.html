A Legacy of Knowledge

Syed Mohammod Minhaz Hossain was a passionate researcher and academician dedicated to the advancement of technology and the growth of his students. He was a vital pillar of the Department of CSE at Premier University.

Beyond the classroom, he was a mentor, serving as the Moderator of the Premier University Debating Society. His dedication earned him the Best Researcher Award 2024.
